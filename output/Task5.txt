Research Question: What percentage of crimes result in an arrest?
Total Crimes: 8517257
Crimes with Arrest: 2140286
Percentage of Crimes Resulting in Arrest: 25.13%
==============================================================================================================================================================================================================================
Mission: The Police Chief needs to assess the efficiency of patrols. How often are criminals actually caught?
the percentage of crimes resulting in an arrest was 25.13%. The results show that there were 2,140,286 cases where an arrest was made and 6,376,971 cases where no arrest occurred.
This shows that criminals are caught in about one out of every four reported crimes. While this shows that law enforcement is able to successfully apprehend suspects in some cases, 
the majority of crimes (around 75%) do not result in an arrest.
